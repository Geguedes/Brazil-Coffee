Mocha

Ingredients

  • Dark Hot Chocolate Flakes
  • Espresso
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Heat 200ml (about one large mug) of milk in a saucepan on the hob, or in a large mug in the microwave. Make sure it does not boil as this will affect the taste.
  2. Add about 8 teaspoons (depending on your taste) of 54% dark hot chocolate flakes and your coffee. Then stir through until your flakes have fully melted.
  3. Pour into a hot chocolate bowl or your favourite mug and enjoy your chocolatey pick-me-up.

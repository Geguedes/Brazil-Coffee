Pumpkin Spice Latte

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin purée
  • Pumpkin pie spice mix
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener
  • Espresso
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Using a high-speed blender, add all the ingredients except for the coffee: pumpkin purée, pumpkin pie spice, cane sugar, vanilla extract, and milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and consistent.
  3. Then pour the hot coffee into the blender and blend again until it is all mixed together.
  4. When it is done blending, it will be smooth, warm, and frothy.
